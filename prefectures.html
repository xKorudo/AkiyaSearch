Japan's 47 prefectures

Japan is divided into 47 prefectures (都道府県), grouped into eight regions from snowy Hokkaidō in the north to tropical Okinawa in the south. Here's a quick guide to each one and what it's famous for — then jump straight to the vacant akiya houses there.

Descriptions are brief summaries for orientation only. Each prefecture links to its Wikipedia article for credible, detailed information; for travel and official data see the Japan National Tourism Organization (JNTO).
