FAQ

Common questions

What does the free trial include?
The 14-day trial gives you full Pro access — all Airbnb estimates, earthquake and tsunami data. No credit card required to start. You'll only be charged if you choose to continue.
Can I cancel at any time?
Yes. Cancel any time from your account — no questions asked. If you cancel during a billing period, you keep Pro access until the end of that period.
How accurate are the Airbnb estimates?
The estimates are based on regional tourism data, property size, location and condition. They are indicative only — not a guarantee of income. Always verify with local market data and a licensed professional.
Where does the earthquake and tsunami data come from?
Earthquake events come from the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS). Tsunami history comes from NOAA's Global Historical Tsunami Database. Both are official, publicly available sources that we query live for each property location.
Is the yearly plan cheaper?
Yes — the yearly plan works out to about €6.60 per month, saving you 30% compared to the monthly plan. Payment is charged once per year.
Can I switch between monthly and yearly?
Yes. You can switch plans at any time from your account settings. If you switch to yearly mid-cycle, we'll prorate the remaining time.
